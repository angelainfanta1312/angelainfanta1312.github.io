If it is meant to be, will it be?

October 14, 2022 One-minute read
Angela
poem â€¢ non-technical

2 years of hard work gave me unexpected results,
No they weren’t positive.
I needed an exotic fruit, but those results gave me rotten smell,
Disappointed me, thought it was unfair.
Never was I ever so stressed,
That I Felt every decision of mine went down the hill,
When all I could hope for is to get some Will.
Now after several months I realize,
Maybe I was insane to choose a harder way,
And that destiny wanted me to shift my lane.
Now that I’m Glad and Grateful for the silver lining I could see through,
I thank all those souls who stood by me and have been through.
Lot of lessons learned on how to stick to the ‘D’s
And not If it’s meant to be, it will be!
For me they are Discipline, Dedication and Devotion
Henceforth never will I ever take any of them Granted,
For now I know how I might get fainted.
And by the end of the day,
I Thank the Master above for all his Play!
